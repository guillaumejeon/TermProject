SERENITY

Not just a game an Experience

Health, tranquility, and peace of mind are the themes for our Serenity. Our team has chosen to create an experience of health and wellbeing. The focus will be on meditation and the proper techniques required to master it. The main version of our application will be accessible using VR devices, and we will be using phones/tablets to add a multi-device feature. In VR, users can access the main meditation experience, and phones/tablets will host an extended experience. The “extended” will include statistics and information to help users track their meditation journey, giving users a feeling of real change.
